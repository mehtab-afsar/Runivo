Runivo turns your city into a battlefield. Every run you complete claims territory — your neighborhood, your streets, your empire. Compete against real runners in your city for control of the map.

TERRITORY WARFARE
Run through streets to claim hexagonal zones. Fortify your territories to make them harder to steal. Attack enemy-held areas by running through them. Watch the live map update as you move.

REAL-TIME MISSIONS
Complete daily and weekly challenges — run 5km before sunrise, claim 3 enemy territories, hit a personal pace record. Earn XP, coins, and rank up your profile.

RUN TRACKING
GPS-accurate route recording with splits, elevation, and pace. Every run is saved to your history and synced with Google Fit / Health Connect.

SOCIAL FEED & STORIES
Share run highlights, post stories from your runs, follow other runners in your city. Like and comment on their territories and routes.

CLUB BATTLES
Form a club with friends and combine your territory for club-vs-club map wars. Join a lobby, chat with clubmates before a run, coordinate attacks.

NUTRITION TRACKING
Log meals and track calories alongside your training. Set daily goals and see how your fuel affects your run performance.

GEAR LOG
Track your running shoes, log mileage per pair, get retirement alerts before they cause injury. Upload photos of each pair.

LEADERBOARD
Weekly city rankings by territory owned, distance run, and XP earned. Monthly resets keep competition fresh.

AI COACH
Ask your AI running coach anything — training plans, injury prevention, race preparation.

RUNIVO+ SUBSCRIPTION
Unlock unlimited territory claims, advanced analytics, priority lobby access, and ad-free experience.

No subscription required to play — claim up to 50 territories and complete all missions for free.
